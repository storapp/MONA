Our Story

Stewardship rooted in institutional discipline

Founded in 2011, MON Asset Management has evolved from a boutique investment firm into one of Australia's premier private wealth management companies. We specialize in serving ultra-high net worth individuals, families, and institutions with sophisticated investment needs.

Our approach combines traditional wealth preservation principles with innovative alternative investment strategies, ensuring our clients' portfolios are well-positioned for long-term growth and capital protection.

With over AUD $500 million in assets under management, we maintain an exclusive client base of fewer than 200 families and institutions, allowing us to provide truly personalized service and attention.

Investment Philosophy

Institutional discipline tailored to private wealth

Long-term Focus

We invest with a long-term horizon, focusing on fundamental value creation rather than short-term market fluctuations.

  • Rigorous analysis and patient capital deployment
  • Multi-generational wealth preservation strategies
  • Value creation through active ownership

Risk Management

Capital preservation is paramount through sophisticated risk management techniques and diversification.

  • Systematic diversification across asset classes
  • Dynamic hedging and volatility overlays
  • Stress testing and scenario modeling

We believe in alternative investments to enhance returns and reduce correlation to public markets.

  • Real estate and infrastructure investments
  • Hedge funds and structured products
